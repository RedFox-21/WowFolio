My Story

Hello! I'm Preston J. Shaw, and I'm thrilled to welcome you to my portfolio. I am a passionate Software Engineer, dedicated to the development and creation of app projects. Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in React Development and Python Programming. I pride myself on my dedicaiton and tenacity on figuring out bugs in code. My goal is to create easier to use, yet still highly effective programs and applications covering a large array of uses. I am constantly seeking opportunities to challenge myself and grow as a professional. I believe in the power of collaboration and enjoy working alongside diverse teams to tackle complex problems. By combining my desire and understanding of programming, I aim to not only build a 'copy and paste' application, but be able to create personal and unique experience with every project! In addition to my professional pursuits, I find inspiration in Video Game Development and Sountrack. These activities fuel my creativity and provide fresh perspectives that I bring into my work. This portfolio showcases a selection of my most notable projects and achievements. Each one represents a unique challenge and a chance for me to apply my expertise. I invite you to explore my work and see how my skills and experiences align with your needs. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to the possibility of collaborating with you.
